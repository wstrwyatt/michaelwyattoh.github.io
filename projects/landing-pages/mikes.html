Who are we?

We are Mike's Cereal Shack and we are cereal lovers! We feature numerous brands of your favorite cereals. We are located in Scranton, Pennsylvania and Boulder, Colorado. Our mission is to provide as many varities of cereal as you can buy and enjoy.

Our company was founded in 2016 by Michael Scott. Michael moved to Boulder, Colorado after leaving Dunder-Mifflin in Scranton, PA. He started working at Dunder-Mifflin in 1994. For a short period he left Dunder-Mifflin to form Michael Scott's Paper Company. This company was later purchased by Dunder-Mifflin. He is also the writer of the star film, Threat-Level Midnight. He and is wife, Holly, has starred in the documentary series The Office: An American Workplace.

Come in and enjoy your favorite cereal!
